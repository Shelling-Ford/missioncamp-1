Program

Small Groups

A time where you meet and have fellowship with new friends and believers from all around the country. There will be small groups for foreigners.


Topical Lectures

Topical lectures will interpret recent events through a biblical perspective and will shed light on the current times. Missionary Paul Choi will speak on the Kingdom of God and God's Vision-how God is redeeming the nations and leading history into completion.


Testimonies

Missionaries will share testimonies of their disciple making and church planting ministries on the mission field.

Elective Lectures

Missionaries will share testimonies of their disciple making and church planting ministries on the mission field.


Media

Experience the global phenome be shown in a biblical perspective and learn what the task of the Korean church is through video.


BTJ Festival

A festival where we lift up the nations through various performances and a Nation Parade proclaiming the BTJ Generation.


Vision and Consulting

One-on-one personal vision counseling with our staff or missionaries are available upon request.
